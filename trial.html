UNITY BONTLE NDIMANDE

BSc Biomathematics · Level 300 · BUAN Sebele Campus

I built this hub because I spent my first week at BUAN running from office to office trying to find basic information about hostels, the cafeteria menu, SRC contacts — things that should just exist somewhere. They didn't. So I built it.

This is a student-to-student resource. No admin jargon, no outdated PDFs buried on a university portal — just real information, kept current, for the people who actually need it. If something's wrong or missing, reach out and I'll fix it.

About DSP

DSP was founded by students who believed leadership should answer directly to the student body — not the other way around. We are agriculture students, business students, science students, sports captains and choir members. We are BUAN.

Our manifesto rests on transparency, accessibility and delivery. Every motion we run will be published. Every Pula in the SRC budget will be tracked. Every promise will be measured.

Past Highlighted Achievements

  • Fought for course accreditation.
  • Successful amendment of BUAN Academic ALMNAC for regional alignment
  • Updating of BUAN Simcards from 1GB to 2.5GB.
  • Partnered with other universities for the establishment of the SRC Union.
  • Introduced faculty student engage forums.
  • Fencing of BUAN Campus.
  • Improving the ITS system.
  • Implemented Field Practical training for level 200 students.

MONEY

Manage your finances

Track every Pula

Write down every expense for one month. You'll be shocked where the money goes — and ready to fix it.

Save first, spend later

Move 20% to savings the day your allowance lands. Treat it as a bill, not an option.

Avoid loan sharks

Interest can hit 30%+. Use the SRC hardship fund first, then registered lenders only.
