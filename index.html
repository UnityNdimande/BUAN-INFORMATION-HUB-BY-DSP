About DSP

DSP was founded by students who believed leadership should answer directly to the student body — not the other way around. We are agriculture students, business students, science students, sports captains and choir members. We are BUAN.

Our manifesto rests on transparency, accessibility and delivery. Every motion we run will be published. Every Pula in the SRC budget will be tracked. Every promise will be measured.

Past HighlightedAchievements

  • Fought for course accreditation.

MONEY

Manage your finances

Track every Pula

Write down every expense for one month. You'll be shocked where the money goes — and ready to fix it.

Save first, spend later

Move 20% to savings the day your allowance lands. Treat it as a bill, not an option.

Avoid loan sharks

Interest can hit 50%+. Use the SRC hardship fund first, then registered lenders only.
